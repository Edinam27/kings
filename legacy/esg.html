Environment · Social · Governance

Energy delivered safely, and with the future in mind.

Our ESG framework sets out how we operate across every desk and terminal — rigorous safety, accountable governance, and investment in the energy transition that is measurable, not decorative.

What the framework means in practice

Three commitments, each owned by a named executive sponsor and reported against annually. We expect the businesses we invest in and trade with to meet the same standards.

E — Environment

Decarbonisation with discipline

We are building renewable capacity, tightening the emissions intensity of the barrels we move, and backing credible carbon markets over symbolic gestures.

S — Social

People and communities first

Safety is non-negotiable. A zero-tolerance standard for unsafe operations extends to every vessel, terminal and counterparty we work with.

G — Governance

Accountable by design

Independent risk oversight, 100% counterparty compliance screening and a whistleblower-protected ethics line sit at the centre of how we deal.

Progress we can point to

Targets on the record, not just on the slide.

We publish the numbers that matter and hold ourselves to them. Where we have not arrived yet, we say so — credible transition planning is honest about the distance still to travel.
